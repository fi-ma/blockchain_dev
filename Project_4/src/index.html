Quick Start

To go successfully from growing coffee cherries to consumer's cup, you need to initiate the actions in the following order:

  2. Pick
  3. Process
  4. Taste
  5. Grade (optional)
  6. Sell
  7. Pack
  8. List
  9. Buy
  10. Ship
  11. Receive
  12. Roast
  13. Offer
  14. Purchase
